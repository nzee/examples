Infosys is in the Computers - Software sector. The current market capitalisation stands at Rs 245,991.29 crore.

The company management includes R Seshasayee - Chairman & Ind.Director, Vishal Sikka - Managing Director & CEO, U B Pravin Rao - Whole Time Director & COO, Jeffrey S Lehman - Independent Director, John W Etchemendy - Independent Director, Kiran Mazumdar Shaw - Independent Director, Punita Kumar Sinha - Independent Director, Ravi Venkatesan - Independent Director, Roopa Kudva - Independent Director.

It is listed on the BSE with a BSE Code of 500209 and the NSE with an NSE Code of INFY.

Its Registered office is at No. 44, Hosur Road, Electronics City, Bengaluru,Karnataka - 560100.

Their Registrars are Karvy Computershare Private Ltd.
